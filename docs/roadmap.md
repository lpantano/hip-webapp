# Evidence Decoded - Community Roadmap

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

---

## 🎯 Our Vision

Evidence Decoded is committed to combating health misinformation through community-driven, evidence-based evaluation of scientific claims. Our roadmap reflects our commitment to transparency, user experience, and scientific integrity.

## 📊 Development Process

We follow an agile development process with:
- **1-week sprint cycles**
- **2-day testing periods** after each sprint
- **Regular community updates** via GitHub and our blog
- **Open issue tracking** - see what we're working on in real-time
- **Community input** - your feedback shapes our priorities
