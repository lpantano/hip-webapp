# Health Integrity Ambassador Program

## Mission
Empower volunteers to combat health misinformation by promoting evidence-based evaluation tools. Ambassadors help communities access reliable health information through healthintegrityproject.org, strengthening public health literacy one conversation at a time.

## What Ambassadors Do
- **Present**: Deliver talks at community groups, workplaces, schools, or local events (15-30 min presentations provided)
- **Share**: Introduce the platform to friends, family, and social networks
- **Distribute**: Place promotional materials in doctor's offices, libraries, community centers, gyms, pharmacies
- **Advocate**: Write letters to local newspapers, blogs, or community publications
- **Connect**: Identify partnership opportunities with health organizations, educators, or community leaders

## How It Works

### Getting Started
1. **Apply**: Submit brief application expressing interest and availability
2. **Onboard**: Complete 90-minute workshop covering platform features, presentation skills, and messaging guidelines
3. **Activate**: Receive ambassador toolkit (presentation slides, promotional materials, talking points)
4. **Engage**: Commit to minimum 2 activities per quarter

### Support & Resources
- Monthly ambassador calls for sharing strategies and Q&A
- Digital asset library (social media graphics, email templates, flyers)
- Direct support channel for questions
- Recognition for high-impact ambassadors

### Annual Renewal
- Brief annual check-in to renew commitment
- Refresher on platform updates
- Opportunity to expand role or mentor new ambassadors

## Time Commitment
Flexible: 2-5 hours per month based on chosen activities
