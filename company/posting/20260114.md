# Personal Repost Version

Hi everyone! I'm thrilled to share something I've been working on for the past 6 months with Lina L. Faller, Ph.D. and Meeta Mistry.

We wanted to contribute to the fight against health misinformation, so we created the Health Integrity Project—a platform designed to bridge the gap between scientific research and the general public.

**Why we built this:**
We all know that simply citing a paper isn't enough to validate a health claim. Context matters. Methodology matters. The full body of evidence matters.

**How we approach it:**
• We use AI to build and scale the platform efficiently
• We leverage AI to enhance the evaluation process
• BUT—there's always a scientist reviewing and validating the information

This is science-backed information, delivered by experts, for everyone.

🔗 Visit https://healthintegrityproject.org/ — Sign up to access the full resource and start exploring evidence-based health evaluations.

Please share this with anyone who might benefit. Together, we can combat misinformation with rigorous, accessible science.

Feedback is always welcome!
