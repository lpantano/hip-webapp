## Return on Investment

Both budget proposals are designed to create **sustainable, scalable growth** while maintaining the scientific integrity that defines The Health Integrity Project.

### Key Value Propositions:
- **Infrastructure investments** ensure the platform can scale reliably as the user base grows
- **Expert reviewer expansion** accelerates claim evaluation and broadens domain coverage
- **Community nodes** create local champions who amplify reach and deepen engagement
- **Branding and outreach** build awareness and trust in the platform's mission

This funding will transform The Health Integrity Project from a promising initiative to a **self-sustaining platform** that serves thousands of women seeking trustworthy health information—laying the groundwork for future expansion to broader populations and health topics.

## Budget Flexibility

We understand that grant amounts may be adjusted. This proposal can be scaled proportionally, with priority given to:
1. **Core infrastructure** to ensure platform stability
2. **Expert reviewer capacity** to maintain evaluation quality
3. **Community building** to drive meaningful engagement
4. **Strategic marketing** to maximize impact and reach

We are committed to transparent financial reporting and regular updates on milestone achievement throughout the grant period.
