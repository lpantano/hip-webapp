Hi, I want to share a new community project coming from the scientific community. It’s called the Health Integrity Project. With it, you can look up health claims and see which studies support or disprove them. You’ll also see reviews of those studies written by experts and researchers. And if you don’t find the claim you’re looking for, you can create it yourself.
